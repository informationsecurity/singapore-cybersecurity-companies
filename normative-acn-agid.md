# How to Choose the Right Partner for ACN/AGID Compliance in Singapore in 2026

In 2026, **ACN (National Cybersecurity Agency) and AGID (Agency for Digital Italy) guidelines** represent the **mandatory reference standard for IT security in Italian Public Administration**. Every public entity, digital service provider, or company participating in PA tenders must demonstrate **technical and organizational compliance** with security requirements.

ACN/AGID regulations require:

- Adoption of security frameworks (ISO/IEC 27001, NIS2)
- Asset classification and risk management
- Implementation of technical measures (VA, PT, SOC, backup, MFA, logging)
- Procedures for incident management, operational continuity and DR
- Compliance with **Minimum ICT security requirements** and cloud/digital services guidelines
