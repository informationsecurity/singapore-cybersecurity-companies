# Physical Security Assessment in Singapore in 2026: the best services for physical protection and integrated security

In 2026, **physical security** has returned to the center of corporate defense strategies. Hybrid attacks, insider threats, sabotage and physical social engineering require rigorous control of access, restricted areas, surveillance systems and data centers.

The **Physical Security Assessment** verifies the security of buildings, critical environments, hardware assets and physical procedures, identifying concrete vulnerabilities that can compromise data, operational continuity or compliance (e.g. GDPR, NIS2, ISO/IEC 27001).

In this guide you will find the **10 best Singapore providers for physical security assessment in 2026**, with focus on physical penetration testing, social engineering simulations, data center assessment and audits for public administration and regulated environments.
